Steve Jobs

Man who Revolutionised the Tech Industry

Here's is time line of Steve Job's Life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco
  • 1973 - Joined Reed College
  • 1975 - Built Apple 1 Computer with Steve Wozniak
  • 1976 - Founded Apple Inc. along with Steve Wozniak and Ron Wayne
  • 1984 - Launched Macintosh at Apple's Annual Shareholder Meeting
  • 1985 - Leaves Apple and starts NeXT
  • 1986 - Buys Pixar Animation Studios
  • 1991 - Marries Laurene Powel
  • 1996 - Apple buys NeXT for $400M
  • 2000 - Back as CEO of Apple
  • 2001 - Introduced First iPod
  • 2005 - Gives commencement speech at Stanford University
  • 2007 - Introduced Iphone, iPod Touch. Apple Computers becomes Apple Inc.
  • 2009 - Jobs takes leave for six months for Liver transplant
  • 2010 - Jobs introduces iPad, iPhone, iPod Touch 4G and MacBook
  • 2011 - Steve Jobs passes away
